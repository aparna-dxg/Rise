About This Event

Welcome to
PUERTO VALLARTA’s
First Variety Talent Contest!

We are so excited to discover and showcase new talent in Puerto Vallarta and are pleased to have PV’s well known entertainment theaters partnering with us to make this happen – Incanto, The Palm and Teatro Vallarta! This talent show is all encompassing and will be revealing ‘soon to be famous’ singers, dancers, comedians, acrobats, and other fabulous talents in a spectacular variety show on February 20th, 2022, at Teatro Vallarta! If you’re ready to launch your career in entertainment – Puerto Vallarta’s the place to do it;click here to sign up for your audition.Space is limited.
