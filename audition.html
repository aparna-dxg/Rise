Auditions And Shows

If you’ve got talent and
are ready to step out
onto the stage,

There Is No Better Place To Do So
Then In Puerto Vallarta – Home To
World-Class Entertainment.

We are looking for talented individuals, duos/trios or groups who want an opportunity to shine and launch their entertainment career in sunny PV.

Audition dates are:
Sundays - November 14, 21 and 28.

We will audition only 90
talented singers, dancers,
comedians, acrobats, and
other fabulous talent to
audition for 36 spots in one
of our talent contest
preliminaries.

Tryouts will be limited to 90
seconds to qualify for
preliminaries.

Contestants must provide their
own props, instruments
excluding piano

A labeled USB flash drive
(performer name and song)
must be provided to the talent
coordinator in advance.
Song lyrics should not include
inappropriate language

Eligibility

Contestants must be new PV
talent; not a current or past paid
performer (3 years) in PV venues

Parental guardian is
required if under 16

You’re ready
to be a star!

About The Show

Preliminaries Will Be Held On
Sundays - January 9, 16, 23, 30, February 6 And 13.
The 6 preliminaries will be hosted by Incanto and
The Palm and the winners of these events will
advance to the FINAL Show.

  • Preliminary acts will be no longer than 3 minutes. Song lyrics should not include inappropriate language
  • Show participants must provide their own props, instruments excluding piano
  • A labeled USB flash drive (performer name and song) must be provided to the talent coordinator in advance. Song lyrics should not include inappropriate language and music should end at the appropriate mark in the song.

All events will have industry judges and
audience-based voting.

Yes, not only will the live audience vote,
but we will live stream and get votes from around the world!
